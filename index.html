San Mateo Elementary School

Informations

  • History of San Mateo Elementary School San Mateo Elementary School was Established in 1952 in a parcel of residential land in Brgy. San Mateo, San Pablo City, It has an area of 2,900 square meters. It was donated by Mr. Nicasio B. Tolentino made last January 31st 1991 with the City Goverment of San Pablo City, Laguna represented by its Mayor Zacarias A. Tizon. The school has Gonzales Type Building with two rooms at the first time of establishment.

San Mateo Elementary School

INFORMATIONS

  • In 1967, Marcos Type Building with three rooms was constructed as additional. Another building named RP-US with three rooms was built in the late 80's. The school covered court was constructed through the help of Congressman Florante "Boy" Aquino in March 1992. In November 1994, Amante Buildingg with two rooms was added through the effort of the Parent-Teacher Community Association (PTCA). The school gate and the fence were concreted for the security and protection of every stakeholder.
